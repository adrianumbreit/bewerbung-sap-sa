The change of perspective

Not an industry switch. A switch of sides.

I'm not moving from industry into IT — I'm changing my perspective on the same problem. Three reasons why that can be an advantage for a Solution Advisor.

01

I was the customer.

At Porsche I worked with the reportings, KPIs and processes that enterprise software maps. I know from my own experience where things get stuck day to day — and I can spot those process hurdles faster in discovery sessions.

02

Familiar means, new context.

The Solution Advisor role thrives on discovery, compelling demos and executive presentations. Pitching at leadership level and building complex reportings for 16 markets are things I know well from my work so far. I bring the core skills with me — now I'm looking forward to the new context.

03

Data skills: solid and hands-on

Through my Business Analytics focus during my master's I learned how to turn data into strategic value. In practice I use SQL, Power BI and business-relevant AI tools to make processes measurable. At SAP I want to apply this data-driven mindset to offer customers the best possible solutions.
